Rugby Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: The goal is to score points by carrying, passing, or kicking the ball into the opponent's goal area.
  • Teams: Each team consists of 15 players (Rugby Union) or 13 players (Rugby League).
  • Scoring: Points are awarded for tries, conversions, penalty goals, and drop goals.
  • Passing: The ball can only be passed backward or sideways.
  • Tackling: Players can tackle opponents to stop their progress, but high tackles are not allowed.
  • Scrum: Used to restart play after minor infractions.
  • Line-out: Used to restart play when the ball goes out of bounds.

Common Violations

  • Forward pass (throwing the ball forward).
  • Knock-on (dropping the ball forward).
  • Offside (being in front of the teammate who last played the ball).
  • High tackle (tackling above shoulder height).
  • Collapsing a scrum.
