Basketball Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: Score points by shooting the ball through the opponentâ€™s hoop.
  • Teams: Each team consists of five players on the court.
  • Scoring: Field goals are worth 2 or 3 points, and free throws are worth 1 point.
  • Dribbling: Players must dribble the ball while moving.
  • Time: Games are played in four quarters, each lasting 10-12 minutes.
  • Shot Clock: Teams must take a shot within 24 seconds.

Common Violations

  • Traveling (moving without dribbling).
  • Double dribble (dribbling, stopping, then dribbling again).
  • Goaltending (blocking a shot on its way down).
  • Fouls (illegal physical contact).
  • Shot clock violation (not shooting within 24 seconds).
