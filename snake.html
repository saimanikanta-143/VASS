Snakes and Ladders Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: Reach the last square (100) before opponents.
  • Players: 2-4 players can participate.
  • Dice Roll: Players roll a six-sided die to move.
  • Ladders: Landing on a ladder base moves the player up.
  • Snakes: Landing on a snake head moves the player down.
  • Winning: The first player to reach square 100 wins.

Common Violations

  • Moving incorrectly after rolling the dice.
  • Skipping ladders or avoiding snakes unfairly.
  • Rolling out of turn.
