Volleyball Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: The goal is to score points by hitting the ball over the net and landing it in the opponent's court.
  • Teams: Each team consists of six players on the court.
  • Scoring: Matches are played in sets, typically best of five, with 25 points per set (must win by 2 points).
  • Serving: The ball is served from behind the end line and must clear the net.
  • Hits: Each team is allowed a maximum of three consecutive touches before returning the ball.
  • Rotations: Players must rotate clockwise when winning the serve.

Common Violations

  • Double hit (same player contacts the ball twice in succession).
  • Carrying or lifting the ball.
  • Touching the net during play.
  • Stepping over the centerline.
  • Blocking or attacking a serve from the front zone.
