Baseball Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: The goal is to score more runs than the opposing team by hitting the ball and running bases.
  • Teams: Each team consists of nine players.
  • Innings: A game consists of nine innings, with each team batting in each inning.
  • Pitching: The pitcher throws the ball towards the batter, who attempts to hit it.
  • Scoring: A run is scored when a player rounds all bases and reaches home plate.
  • Outs: A batter can be out by striking out, being caught out, or being tagged out.

Common Fouls

  • Foul ball (ball hit outside the field boundaries).
  • Strikeout (missing three swings at a pitch).
  • Interference (obstructing a fielder's play).
  • Illegal bat usage.
  • Base running violations.
