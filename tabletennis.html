Table Tennis Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: Score 11 points before the opponent with at least a 2-point lead.
  • Players: The game can be played in singles (1v1) or doubles (2v2).
  • Service: The ball must be tossed at least 16 cm and struck behind the end line.
  • Scoring: A player scores a point when the opponent fails to return the ball properly.
  • Let Serve: If the ball touches the net on service but lands correctly, the serve is replayed.
  • Game Format: Matches are typically best of 5 or 7 games.

Common Violations

  • Illegal serve (not tossing the ball high enough).
  • Double bounce (ball bounces twice before return).
  • Edge ball (hitting the side of the table without bouncing).
  • Obstructing the ball (touching it before it bounces on your side).
