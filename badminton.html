Badminton Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: Score 21 points before the opponent with at least a 2-point lead.
  • Players: The game can be played in singles (1v1) or doubles (2v2).
  • Service: The shuttlecock must be hit below waist height and land in the diagonal service box.
  • Scoring: A player scores a point when the opponent fails to return the shuttlecock properly.
  • Faults: Hitting the shuttlecock outside the boundary, touching the net, or double hitting.
  • Game Format: Matches are typically best of 3 games.

Common Violations

  • Serving above waist height.
  • Shuttlecock landing outside the court.
  • Double contact with the shuttlecock.
  • Obstructing the opponent.
