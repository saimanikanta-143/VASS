‚öΩ Football Rules

1. Two teams compete to score more goals than the opponent within 90 minutes.
2. Each team starts with 11 players (including the goalkeeper).
3. A match consists of two halves of 45 minutes each with a 15-minute halftime.
4. The match starts with a kickoff from the center circle.
5. The ball is in play unless it completely crosses the goal line or touchline.
6. Direct free kick: Can be taken directly toward the goal.
7. Indirect free kick: Another player must touch the ball before a goal can be scored.
