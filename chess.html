Chess Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: Checkmate the opponent's king.
  • Players: The game is played between two players.
  • Board Setup: Each player starts with 16 pieces: 1 king, 1 queen, 2 rooks, 2 knights, 2 bishops, and 8 pawns.
  • Moves: Each type of piece moves in a specific pattern.
  • Checkmate: The game ends when a king is under attack and cannot escape.
  • Draws: Stalemate, threefold repetition, insufficient material, or agreement can result in a draw.

Common Violations

  • Illegal move (moving a piece in an invalid manner).
  • Touch-move rule (if you touch a piece, you must move it if legal).
  • Insufficient material (no possible way to checkmate).
  • Flag fall (exceeding the time limit in a timed game).
