Cricket Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: Score more runs than the opposing team.
  • Teams: Each team consists of 11 players.
  • Innings: A team bats while the other bowls and fields; roles swap after the innings.
  • Runs: Scored by running between the wickets or hitting boundaries (4 or 6 runs).
  • Outs: A batsman can be out by bowled, caught, leg before wicket (LBW), run out, or stumped.
  • Overs: A limited-overs match consists of a fixed number of overs per innings (e.g., 20, 50).

Common Violations

  • No ball (illegal delivery, e.g., overstepping the crease).
