Bowling Rules & Regulations

Basic Rules

  • Objective: Knock down as many pins as possible in ten frames.
  • Players: Bowling can be played individually or in teams.
  • Scoring: A game consists of ten frames, and players roll twice per frame unless they score a strike.
  • Strike: Knocking down all ten pins in the first roll earns a strike.
  • Spare: Knocking down all pins in two rolls earns a spare.
  • Foul: Stepping beyond the foul line results in a zero score for that roll.

Common Violations

  • Stepping over the foul line.
  • Using an illegal ball.
  • Not adhering to turn order.
